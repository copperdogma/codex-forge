Alma Marie (L'Heureux) Alain

Henry and Alma Alain on their 50th wedding anniversary in 1957.

Alma was the eldest child of Moise and Sophie L'Heureux, born July 31, 1893, in Winnipeg, Manitoba. At the age of about 3 she travelled with her parents to the Domremy district in Saskatchewan.

Alma married Henry Alain on April 8, 1907 at Domremy. Together they raised a family of four boys and four girls on their farm in the Domremy area.

Henry and Alma celebrated their 50th wedding anniversary in 1957, surrounded by their children, grandchildren and many friends and relatives from the district.
