GEORGE L'HEUREUX

George L'Heureux was born on January 6, 1891, at Chateau Richer, Quebec. He was the sixth child and fourth son of Moise and Sophie L'Heureux. George came west with the family as an infant when they made the journey to Saskatchewan in 1891.

George married Emilienne Couture, his first wife. They settled on a farm in the Vawn district. After Emilienne's passing, George married Sophranie, and together they continued to work the land and raise their family.

George was known throughout the district as a skilled farmer and a generous neighbour. He took an active part in community affairs and was well respected by all who knew him. George and his family contributed greatly to the development of the area.
