LEONIDAS L'HEUREUX

Leonidas L'Heureux was born in the Crows Nest Pass in the Rocky Mountains in 1885. He was the third child of Moise and Sophie L'Heureux. As a young man, Leonidas worked in the lumber camps and on the farm.

Leonidas married Laetitia Gagne on June 9, 1906. They had four children together before Laetitia passed away on May 10, 1910. The children from this first marriage were Ida Julia, Alice, Alma, and Cecile.

On April 18, 1915, Leonidas married Josephine Dion. Together they had twelve more children: Marguerite, Julien, Louisa, Edouard, Ralph, Emile, Marie Rene, Moise, Blanche, Leonard, Rita, and Lucienne.

Leonidas farmed in the Vawn district for many years. He was known as a hard-working man who provided well for his large family. He passed away on July 6, 1957.
